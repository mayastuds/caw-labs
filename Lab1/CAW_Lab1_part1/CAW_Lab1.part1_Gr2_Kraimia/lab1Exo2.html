Main Content

Since October 7, 2023, the Gaza Strip has suffered devastating losses. More than 40,000 Palestinians have been killed, including over 16,000 children and 10,000 women. Tens of thousands have been injured, and entire families have been wiped out. The destruction includes hospitals, schools, mosques, and residential buildings. (Sources: Sehatty.ps, Al Jazeera Report)

The blockade has left civilians without food, water, electricity, or medical supplies. Thousands are displaced and forced to live in makeshift shelters while entire neighborhoods lie in ruins.

The war has destroyed much of Gaza’s infrastructure, and the humanitarian situation continues to deteriorate.
