Brief History

The land known today as Palestine has been inhabited for millennia and was a crossroads of ancient civilizations.

During the 20th century the region experienced major political change; the Palestinian national movement grew in response to colonialism and the events surrounding 1948, shaping its modern history.

Today, Palestinians continue to pursue self-determination and a peaceful resolution to longstanding conflicts while preserving their culture and heritage.
